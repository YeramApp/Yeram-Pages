YERAM

All Things Armenian, Right at Your Fingertips.

Yeram is your gateway to the Armenian diaspora, offering you the ability to connect, explore, and engage with businesses, cultural sites, and events all in one place. Whether you're looking to discover Armenian culture near you, find local businesses, or stay updated on community happenings, Yeram makes it easy. From traveling and shopping to connecting with your community, Yeram brings everything Armenian right to your fingertips.

What is Yeram?

There is an obvious parallel between the strength of Armenia and Armenian Diaspora. With a more united, richer and developing Armenian Diaspora, Armenia can become a stronger player on the international arena. The idea of Yeram is to provide a platform that will unite the Armenian Diaspora by showcasing local Armenian entities (restaurants, shops, cafes, etc) as well as local Armenian experts. We aim to encourage Armenians abroad to buy from, work with and initiate more projects with other Armenians from Diaspora. We strive to launch this initiative in every city in the world to benefit Armenians and Armenian businesses worldwide.

Stronger diaspora, stronger country!

Knowing each other strengthens our bonds. Yeram connects Armenian businesses, cultural sites, and events, building deeper understanding and unity within the community.

Innovative

Our systems utilize state-of-the-art technology. By implementing innovative features, we ensure that Armenian businesses, cultural sites, and events are more accessible and interconnected than ever before.

Community-Driven

Built by and for the Armenian community, Yeram operates as a non-profit initiative. It reflects our shared values and goals, promoting unity and collaboration across the globe without commercial interests.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We’ve Got Answers.
Here, you’ll find answers to some common questions. Not finding what you’re looking for? Feel free to ask us directly using the form below.

Is the app free to use?

Yes, the app is completely free for users. We operate as a non-profit organization with the goal of connecting and strengthening the Armenian community. All without any cost to our users.

I’m a community leader or know many organizations in my city, but my city isn’t listed in the app. How can I add my city?

We’re thrilled that you want to contribute! We’d love for you to become an ambassador for your area and help us bring your city into the app! Please send us your contact details via contact@yeram.info and we’ll get in touch with you shortly to discuss the next steps.

I’m a Business Owner and Want My Business to Be Featured in Your App. What Should I Do?

We’re excited that you want to be part of our community! You can easily submit your business information directly through the app or via the form below (Alternatively write us an E-mail at contact@yeram.info).
Please provide your business name, address, and a brief description of the type of business you run. We’ll get in touch with you as quickly as possible. Registration is completely free of charge.

What's up with this color scheme?

We chose purple for our app inspired by its significance in the Armenian Apostolic Church, where it symbolizes penance, humility, and royalty. This color reflects our commitment to creating a thoughtful and respectful environment, while also embodying a sense of dignity and depth. Just as purple is used during solemn occasions to prepare for renewal, we hope our color scheme enhances your experience and encourages reflection.

My Local Community or Church Is Missing. What Can I Do?

Please provide the name, location, and any relevant information. If you'd like us to get in touch with you, please also leave your contact details.
